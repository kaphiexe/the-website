# 08/09/2025 Please be quiet, Dim the sounds in my mind. I don’t read around my friends during Wednesday reading because I claim that I won’t fall for that propaganda, I won’t let Steve get his way. But the truth is I would love some time to read, any day. I read out loud. I read with emotion. Which is why I can’t read with anyone around. I need sound. Sometimes the words get jumbled up. I don’t care; it makes sense to me. That’s really all that words are supposed to be. Please stop the chanting. Please stop the squeal. Please let me heal. When I am alone, I talk to myself. Not just to myself, I talk to my friends as well. Some of my friends are real, and some are fake. Which is why I find reality hard to grasp. I can still taste their flavours. Sour, salty, sweet, or heat. I found out that most people don’t have flavours. I thought we did just no one talked about it, that much. It’s subtle anyways so I don’t really care one bit. I am not crazy. I don’t think that at all. Even if I say that I am not small. My words don’t make much sense. Two hours of my life so gone, I wonder what I’ll do next wrong. I feel very safe with my life right now. I have a good relationship with food. I don’t overwork myself. I enjoy the little things in life. I wake up every morning and say “Life is too beautiful to not smile everyday.” And so I do. I smile everyday. Everyone is lying to me. So I don’t believe anyone, but myself, anymore. I don’t plan on keeping score. When I go on my walks, me and my friends, we talk. I went to tell someone about it, but then I realised the old lady I made friends with was in my head. I had a nice time talking to her, I hope she did as well. I won’t be confined to a clock or a bell. Just like that I won't be confined to this reality, my mind can take me some place else. I wonder what was evil this morning… Why did I wake up sweating with my heart racing? That happens a lot, so it's not unordinary. But, why the evil chants? Is it me? Am I evil? These grapes that I munch have a very nice crunch.
